# Personalized Marketing That Feels Human

Most restaurant owners we speak with are skeptical of "AI marketing." They've seen generic emails and robotic chatbots that drive customers away.

The difference is in doing it right.

A local Italian restaurant in Marlborough implemented a practical system that combines their existing POS data with simple AI to send the right message at the right time.

**Results:**
- 29% increase in repeat visits within 60 days
- 41% higher average ticket from targeted offers
- Customers saying the emails "feel like they really know me"

The system knows when someone hasn't visited in 3 weeks, what they usually order, and what new items might appeal to them. It then crafts messages in the owner's own voice.

This isn't replacing human connection — it's scaling it.
