# Predictive Inventory That Cut Food Waste by 37% at a Marlborough Bistro

Every restaurant owner knows the pain of throwing away perfectly good ingredients at the end of the night. For many independent eateries in the Marlborough area, food waste can eat 30%+ of their margin.

One local bistro we worked with implemented a practical predictive inventory system that delivered immediate results.

## The Problem
- Inconsistent foot traffic based on weather, local events, and day of week
- Over-ordering from suppliers to "be safe"
- Manual guessing leading to $2,800/month in spoiled inventory

## The Practical AI Solution
We built a simple system that pulls:
- Historical sales data
- Weather forecasts
- Local event calendar
- Day-of-week patterns

It recommends exact order quantities 3 days in advance.

**Results after 90 days:**
- Food waste reduced by 37%
- $2,100 average monthly savings
- Better cash flow (less money tied up in excess inventory)
- Staff spends 4 fewer hours per week on ordering

The best part? It runs on a simple spreadsheet + free AI tools — no expensive software required.
