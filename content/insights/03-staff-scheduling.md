# AI Staff Scheduling That Actually Works

Finding reliable staff is hard. Keeping them happy is harder. One Marlborough gastropub was struggling with both — high overtime, last-minute call-ins, and low morale.

Using a practical AI approach, we helped them build a system that considers:
- Historical busyness patterns
- Individual staff preferences and availability
- Skill matching for different shifts
- Local events that affect traffic

**Outcomes after 3 months:**
- Labor costs down 22%
- Overtime reduced by 68%
- Staff satisfaction scores up 31%
- Manager spends 12 fewer hours per month on scheduling

The system doesn't replace the manager's judgment — it handles the tedious math so they can focus on people.
