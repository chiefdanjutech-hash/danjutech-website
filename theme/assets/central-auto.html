Why it matters

The Independent Difference

Most Shops Answer to Your Insurer — We Answer to You

Most local shops participate in insurer-run Direct Repair Programs (DRPs). That means their first obligation is to the insurance company — not the car owner. Central Auto Rebuilders has never joined a DRP and never will. Our expertise is used exclusively to fight for your repair, your rights, and your vehicle being restored to pre-accident condition.

Your advocate, full stop

We review your claim, challenge shortcuts, and make sure your vehicle is restored to pre-accident condition — not just repaired to the minimum the insurer will approve.

Insurance navigation included

We handle the paperwork, communicate directly with your insurer, and coordinate rental cars — so you can focus on getting back to your life.

Craft over throughput

Every job is handled by experienced technicians who care about the result. We're not a high-volume DRP shop chasing quotas — we're a repair shop that takes pride in the work.

The only independent shop in Marlborough

Every other registered auto body shop in the area participates in DRP agreements. We are the only one that doesn't — by choice, on principle, since 1957.

Our story

Nearly 70 Years of Showing Up for Marlborough

Central Auto Rebuilders has been operating at 53 Central Street since 1957 — longer than most businesses in this city have existed. Incorporated in Massachusetts in 1961, the shop has served generations of Marlborough drivers through every kind of collision, every kind of insurance dispute, and every kind of repair.

The shop has earned the Greater Marlborough Chamber of Commerce Small Business of the Year award and has maintained deep community ties through youth sports sponsorships, civic organizations, and local events — year after year, decade after decade.

Today the business remains independently owned and operated, holding a Massachusetts Repair Shop License and an A+ BBB rating with zero complaints on record. The commitment to independent, customer-first service is the same as it's always been.
